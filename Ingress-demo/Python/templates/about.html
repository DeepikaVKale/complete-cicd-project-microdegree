About Naruto Uzumaki

Born: Naruto Uzumaki was born as the son of the Fourth Hokage, Minato Namikaze, and his wife, Kushina Uzumaki. He was born into a world filled with conflict and hardship.

Life: Naruto's life was marked by isolation as he was shunned by his village, the Hidden Leaf, due to the Nine-Tails Fox sealed within him. Despite this, Naruto always dreamed of becoming the Hokage to gain the respect of his village.
